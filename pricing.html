Prices are per unit, billed monthly, with a minimum per plan. Each plan includes staff seats (2 / 6 / 15) covering every role — owner, admins, office and maintenance — and any extra staff seat is $3/month; resident accounts are always unlimited and free. Online payment collection is an optional add-on: $9/month, plus a flat $2 per bank payment (paid by the building; free to the resident); card payments carry a 3.5% + $0.35 fee paid by the resident. Available on any plan. An optional AI Agent add-on ($29/month, 1,500 resident messages included, then $15 per additional 1,500) answers residents 24/7, opens maintenance tickets and explains charges.
