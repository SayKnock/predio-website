Payments

Predio Payments

$9/building/month + $2 per bank payment

Collect rent and dues in-app. Residents pay by bank transfer or card, with autopay and instant receipts. Where online pay isn't available, a resident reports a bank payment with a receipt photo and the building confirms it. Card fee 3.5% + $0.35, paid by the resident.

AI

AI Agent

$29/building/month · 1,500 messages per building

A bilingual assistant inside resident chat, 24/7. It answers questions, opens maintenance tickets, and explains charges from real data — with a person able to take over anytime.
